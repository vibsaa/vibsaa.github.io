01. About Me

I'm currently advancing accelerator architecture for ML workloads at MARS Lab, Washington State University. Previously, I worked as a Senior Silicon Design Engineer at AMD in the power management team, verifying core and IP-level functionality of AMD's processors.

With over 2 years of experience in design verification using tools like Xilinx Vivado, VC Formal, Verdi, Verilog, and SystemVerilog, I bring a strong background in embedded systems and VLSI with a Master's degree from Netaji Subhas Institute of Technology.
